About OKLA Pakistan

OKLA join hands with the Ravi Sundar to set up its first assembling facility outside of China. Ravi has been associated with the automotive assembly & manufacturing sector since the 1960s. In addition, it has investments in manufacturing, distribution, real estate & the service sectors. This alliance aims to make Pakistan their manufacturing hub for EVs in the region & beyond.
