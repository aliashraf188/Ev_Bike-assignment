OKT EV Bike

The Okla OKT is an affordable, practical, and efficient electric bike/scooter manufactured by Okla Pakistan for city commutes, featuring a 1000W motor, a maximum speed of 50 km/h, and a range of up to 110 km on a single charge. It comes with a Graphene non-lithium battery, front and rear disc brakes, a 2-year motor warranty, and an 18-month battery warranty, making it a reliable and cost-effective option for daily transportation in Pakistan.
